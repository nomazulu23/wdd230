Target Audience

The target audience are European Hospitals, registered nurses, student nurses, nurse aids and residents in Lublin

Personas

Student nurses

Nurse Care helps students nurses by linking them to different organisation and institutions that can help them get jobs or advance their educational careers. Student nurse can look for a variety of project within Poland and Europe at large.

Registered Nurses

Nurse care exposes all career avenues for registered nurses that want to move to greener pastures or explore options of advancing their qualification. They will interact with different organisation in the health field in Poland and Europe at large.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

A student who is worried about finding a job after graduation can make use of the Nurse Care chamber of commerce to search and interact with different organisation. They can get information on how to get employment locally or in other countries. The organisation will have detailed information on expectations and qualifications needed. For instance, a student may get in contact with hospitals and recruitment agents around Europe to get a job and practice a nurse.

Scenario 2

Students who would like to advance their education levels have the opportunity to interact with health institution that offer different post-graduate qualifications especially if they want to specialise and not be a general nurse.

Scenario 3

Registered nursed in Poland who want to go practice in other countrues have the opportunity to link up with companies that recruit registered nurses to areas of demand in Europe.

Scenario 4

Registered general nurses who wish to advance their career abd become radiologist, surgical nurses, internal medicine nurses and Nurse anesthetist, Nurse Educator, Ocology Nurse, Public Health nurse, Pediatric nurse, Travel nurse, and midwife to mention a few have the opportunity to get the know how of requirement and assistance to relocate.

Scenario 5
